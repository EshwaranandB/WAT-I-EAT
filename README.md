# 🍱 WAT-I-EAT: The Health Architect
### *Turning every Indian meal into a data-driven health masterpiece.*

**WAT-I-EAT** isn't just a calorie counter. It is a **6-Act Narrative Health Engine** designed for the Indian sub-continent. Built on the raw power of **AMD AI Inference** and **Google Gemini 2.5 Flash**, it transforms a simple photo of your plate into a clinical health diagnostic, a regional thali optimizer, and a metabolic energy predictor.

---

## 🎬 The 11-Act Narrative Workflow

### 🎭 Phase 1: The Personalization Matrix
*   **Act 01: The Hero Hook** – A stunning, glassmorphic landing designed to "wow" judges within 3 seconds.
*   **Act 02: Profile Setup** – Hyper-regional calibration (Budget, Mood, Region, Medical conditions).
*   **Act 1.5: The Health Pulse** – Real-time circular progress rings tracking your daily Calorie, Protein, and Fiber intake.

### 🍱 Phase 2: The Scanning Magic
*   **Act 03: Snap & Know** – Ultra-fast food identification using Gemini 2.5 Flash with custom scanning Lottie animations.
*   **Act 04: Intelligence Cards** – 3D Ingredient Flip-Cards that reveal "Health Superpowers" (e.g., Turmeric's anti-inflammatory properties).
*   **Kitchen Intelligence** – AI-generated "Recipe Hacks" to turn unhealthy Indian meals into nutritious versions (e.g., Air-frying Samosas).

### 🩺 Phase 3: The Clinical Vault
*   **Act 07: Micronutrient Vault** – Deep-dive analysis of 7 essential Vitamins and Minerals (A, B12, C, D, Iron, Zinc, Calcium).
*   **Act 10: Metabolic Insight** – A high-fidelity Plotly chart predicting your **4-hour Energy & Glucose Curve** post-meal.

### 🛵 Phase 4: Power Ecosystem
*   **Act 08: The Social Pulse** – A regional leaderboard and 3-day streak tracker showing community nourishments.
*   **Act 09: Architect Report** – One-click export of your daily nutritional footprint for clinical sharing.
*   **Act 11: Leftover Alchemist** – Turn your fridge "Odds and Ends" into healthy, zero-waste Indian meals.

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack of Tomorrow

| Layer | Technology | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Logic Engine** | **Google Gemini 2.5 Flash** | OCR, Nutritional Inference, and Recipe Alchemy. |
| **Frontend** | **Streamlit** | Rapid, reactive UI deployment. |
| **Styling** | **Nuclear Glassmorphism (CSS)** | 600+ lines of custom Vanilla CSS to hide the "Streamlit look". |
| **Analytics** | **Plotly & SVG** | Interactive Radar charts, Glucose curves, and Progress rings. |
| **Animations** | **Lottie (BodieMovin)** | High-fidelity vector animations for scanning feedback. |
| **Optimization** | **Pillow** | Memory-resident image thumbnailing for ultra-low latency inference. |
