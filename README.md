## 🚀 The Vision: Beyond Calorie Counting
Most nutrition apps are built for Western diets, counting salads and smoothies. **WAT-I-EAT** is built for the chaos, the culture, and the constraints of the 1.4 billion people in India. 

From the ₹20 Vada Pav on the street to the home-cooked comfort of Maa's Dal Thali, WAT-I-EAT doesn't just "see" food—it understands the **story of why you're eating it.** 

Developed during the **AMD Slingshot Regional Ideathon 2025**, this isn't just an app; it’s a high-performance **behavioral AI intervention** designed to tackle India's growing disease burden via world-class nutritional intelligence.

---

## 💎 The 5-Act Experience

### 🎭 Act 1: The Hook (Hyper-Personalization)
Forget generic advice. WAT-I-EAT profiles your **native region**, **budget constraints**, **medical conditions**, and even your **current mood** to ensure every recommendation feels like it's coming from a world-class nutritionist who lives in your hometown.

### 📸 Act 2: Snap & Know (The Magic)
Powered by the state-of-the-art **Google Gemini 2.5 Flash** model, WAT-I-EAT provides a comprehensive **Dish DNA Analysis** in seconds:
- **NourishScore™**: A 0-100 proprietary health grade.
- **Macronutrient Tracking**: Real-time visualization of Protein, Carbs, Fats, and Fiber.
- **Actionable Verdicts**: "Great for your PCOS," "Watch out for hidden sodium," or "Swap this roti for a bajra roti."

### 🛵 Act 3: Swiggy Interceptor
Stuck on what to order? Paste any restaurant menu and WAT-I-EAT will rank every dish based on **your specific body goal**. It turns a 50-item menu into a 1-item decision.

### 😤 Act 4: The Mood-Food Engine
Eating is emotional. Our AI detects neuro-food cravings based on your stress or motivation levels and suggests healthy Indian alternatives that satisfy the brain without destroying the gut.

### 🏠 Act 5: Regional Thali Builder
The "Holy Grail" of budget eating. Specify your daily limit (e.g., ₹150) and your region (e.g., Gujarat), and WAT-I-EAT constructs a **chemically balanced 4-meal daily plan** using local staples.

## 🚦 Getting Started (Dev Mode)

1. **Clone the Source of Truth**:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/EshwaranandB/WAT-I-EAT.git
   cd WAT-I-EAT
   ```

2. **Configure Your Identity**:
   Create a `.env` file and drop your secret key:
   ```env
   GOOGLE_API_KEY=your_gemini_api_key_here
   ```

3. **Ignite the Server**:
   ```bash
   pip install -r requirements.txt
   streamlit run app.py
   ```
